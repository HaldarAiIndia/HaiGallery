This Privacy Policy explains how HaiGallery ("the App"), developed by HaldarAi, collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to full transparency and to handling your data responsibly. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

01

Interpretation & Definitions

App – the HaiGallery mobile application published by HaldarAi.

Personal Data – any information that can be used, alone or combined, to identify a natural person.

Usage Data – anonymous, aggregated information collected automatically when you use the App (e.g., crash reports, feature interaction counts).

Device – any smartphone, tablet, or other device you use to access the App.

We / Us / Our – HaldarAi, the developer and data controller of the App.

02

Data We Collect

Media on Your Device

The App reads your photos and videos from local storage to display, organise, and manage them. Your media never leaves your device and is never uploaded to our servers. Associated metadata (creation date, embedded GPS, filename) is processed only on-device to power search and organisation features.

Device & Usage Information

We may collect non-identifiable device information — model, operating system version — and anonymous usage signals such as crash reports and feature-use counts. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you individually.

03

How We Use Your Data

Core gallery services — display, organise, edit, tag, and manage your photos and videos on-device.
App improvement — understand how features are used; fix bugs and crashes via Firebase analytics.
Performance monitoring — detect and resolve technical issues to ensure a smooth experience.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

04

Retention Periods

Your media and metadata remain on your device under your full control — we hold no copies. Anonymous analytics data collected via Firebase is retained for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted in accordance with Google's data-retention policies. Crash reports are purged once the underlying issue is resolved or within 90 days, whichever comes first.

05

Data Transfers

The App is operated from West Bengal, India. Anonymous analytics data may be processed on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside India. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that any third-party processors provide an adequate level of data protection consistent with applicable law, including the Indian Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

06

Your Rights & Deletion

Under applicable privacy laws (including the DPDPA) you have the right to:

• Access — request information about what data we hold about you.
• Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected.
• Deletion — request erasure of your personal data from our systems.
• Withdrawal of consent — opt out of analytics at any time by disabling internet permission for the App in your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at business.tizongaming@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Disclosure of Data

We may disclose information in the following limited circumstances:

• Legal obligation — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
• Protection of rights — to protect the safety, rights, or property of HaldarAi or its users.
• Service providers — to trusted third parties (e.g., Firebase/Google) who process data solely on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.

We use Firebase (Google) for crash analytics and app stability. Firebase may collect anonymous device information. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

08

Security

All media data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. For the minimal anonymous analytics data that passes through Firebase, Google employs industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. While no method of electronic storage is 100 % secure, we apply reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your information. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware.

09

Children's Privacy

HaiGallery is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately. We will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.

10

Third-Party Links & Services

The App may contain links to external websites or use third-party libraries. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party service. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of every third-party service you interact with. The key third-party components used in the App are:

• Firebase (Google) — analytics & crash reporting.
• Coil — on-device image loading and caching (no external transmission).
• Retrofit & OkHttp — networking architecture for any future secure API calls.
